How it works

Three steps from question to output

01

Pick a tool and a domain

Choose one of nine tools, then lock the conversation to a scientific domain so answers stay on-topic.

02

Describe the task

Paste an abstract, name a topic, or state two theories to compare — plain language is enough.

03

Get a structured result

Granite returns Markdown-formatted output with clear sections, ready to copy into your own work.
