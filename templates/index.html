Research Chat — Physics Domain
What is quantum entanglement?
Quantum entanglement is a phenomenon where two particles become correlated so that the quantum state of one instantly influences the other, regardless of distance.

This non-local correlation defies classical physics and is foundational to quantum computing and quantum cryptography.
